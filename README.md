Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard — AdventureWorks
Tool: Power BI Desktop | Dataset: AdventureWorks (Maven Analytics)
---
Project Overview
Built this end-to-end Power BI report to develop real-world business intelligence skills — covering data modelling, DAX measures, and interactive report design using a retail sales dataset.
The report answers three core business questions:
How is the business performing overall (revenue, profit, orders, returns)?
Which products are driving or dragging performance?
Who are the most valuable customers and what drives their behaviour?
---
Dashboard Pages
Page	What It Shows
Exec Dashboard	KPI cards (Revenue, Profit, Orders, Return Rate), Revenue Trending, Orders by Category, Top 10 Products
Map	Geographic distribution of orders
Product Detail	Drill-through page — monthly Orders/Revenue/Profit vs. Target, price adjustment impact, AI report summary
Customer Detail	17.4K customers segmented by income & occupation, Top 100 Customers table, Revenue per Customer trend
Category Tooltip	Weekly orders trend shown as interactive tooltip on category visuals
Q&A	Natural language query page
Decomposition Tree	Root cause breakdown of key metrics
Key Influencers	AI visual identifying drivers of high/low return rates

Technical Skills Applied
Data Modelling — built relationships across Sales, Customer, Product, Calendar, and Territory tables
DAX Measures — rolling revenue, adjusted profit, return rate ratios, MoM % change, and dynamic KPI comparisons
Drill-Through — product-level deep dive from summary to detail page
Tooltips — custom category tooltip page triggered on hover
Cross-filtering — slicers and visuals linked across pages for interactive exploration
AI Visuals — Key Influencers and Decomposition Tree for automated insight generation
